The Biggest Flops of the 2000s: Ranked

#1 Sonic The Hedgehog (2006)
Whilst having an incredible amount of potential
"Sonic 06" never really succeeded at amazing
audiences. Glitches and dumb story plotlines
offput audiences everywhere.
#2 Tomb Raider: Angel of Darkness (2003)
Dumb AI, poorly-aged graphics and overall enjoyability,
or lack of, places this game at no 2 this list.
#3 GTA 2 (1999)
GTA 2 was not necessarily a bad game, however
considering Rockstar Game's catalogue
and skills they should have done better here.
It is practically the same game as the first.
#4 Bomberman: Act Zero (2006)
Bomberman is already a stressful enough experience,
so when the game is lazy, as well as
having a "first person mode" in third
person it can really ruin the game.
